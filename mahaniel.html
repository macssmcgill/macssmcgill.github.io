Vice Presidents Academic Daniel da Costa and Maha Nagaria

Daniel da Costa

Hey Anatomy!

My name is Daniel da Costa, I’m a U3 Anatomy and Cell Biology Student, and I am one of your VP Academics this year. Our goals are to enhance academic and research opportunities available for Anatomy Undergraduates, and make your academic lives much easier in the coming year in Anatomy and Cell Biology.

Although I don’t have any direct experience working on council as my counterpart Maha does, I have worked in other councils and committees throughout my academic career and am currently working as the coordinator of the Patient Intake and Research Assistant Training Program at Agoo Pediatric Medical Center in Laval where I spearhead the McGill volunteer recruitment. This puts me in a unique position to understand what it takes to get those research opportunities and how important it is to get involved.

Come say hi when I’m at the office, I love meeting new people, especially fellow anatomists!

-Daniel

Maha Nagaria

Hey hey Anatomy! My name is Maha Nagaria, one very lucky and happy VP Academic of your department’s student society! I’m in my 3rd year but because I come from the west coast (#bestcoast) I’m a U2 in Anat! Dan and I are set this year to make your academic experience (which is most of your experience at McGill) as effortless and productive as possible! We’re strong believers in studying smart over studying hard and that’s why we’ll be there to help give you that extra edge!

I have been on council as your U1 Representative last year and am familiar with the nooks and crannies of the ol’ Strathcona building, but beyond that I can be your go-to gal for much, much more! I’m an avid thespian, hiker, journalist, teacher, debater, and clinical volunteer! If there is anything I have learned at McGill so far is that the more you express yourself, the more you grow, and the greater the impact you make in your community! Chances are you’ll see me around campus but always feel free to drop by our office to discuss your courses (our primary mandate), life, med apps, research, entertainment, food (which is life), politics, anything!

-Maha

You can contact Dan and Maha at macss.academic@gmail.com.
